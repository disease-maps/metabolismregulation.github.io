Welcome to the Metabolism Regulation Project, an effort focused on describing the connections between signalling and metabolic networks important for the regulation of metabolic processes, their role in health and disease.

The project is desiged as an open community framework: any student, researcher, group can join and contribute.

To learn more about the project please review the gallery of the available diagrams developed in Systesm Biology Graphical Notation (SBGN, Le Novere, 2009, PMID ). There is a list of selected topics that are already in work or prepared for curation.
